"And at last I see the light..."

Established in 2015 and based in the heart of Kent, our candle workshop is always busy creating candles that will bring a little light to our clientele.

All our non-scented votives are handpoured and crafted by the expert hands of our head chandler,
Melanie Freeman, and we take pride in providing the friendly, helpful service to match our beautiful candles. We specialize in festive and Disney votives and love nothing more than creating something extra special for a particular event.
Safety is paramount and each new candle and holder is tested several times before we begin making a new batch.
